What are Objectives and Key Results?

By using Objectives and Key Results you will be able to make your organization work focused towards common goals. An Objective is what you would like to achieve, while Key Results are what you need to measure to reach the objective. Combine these and you have an OKR. PureOKRs aligns all OKRs in your organization, assigns them to employees and let's you measure your progress.
