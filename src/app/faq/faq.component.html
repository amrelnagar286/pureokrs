Frequently Asked Questions

What is Objectives and Key Results (OKRs)?

It is a goal setting methodology for operationalizing a company's startegy. Objectives are main goals, while key results are what you need to accomplish to reach the main goals. OKRs are organized in a tree structure starting with high level OKRs which are broken down into smaller and smaller OKRs as the tree is traversed. The methodology is used by all kinds of companies - from small tech startups to industrial giants.
